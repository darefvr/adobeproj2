THE HAVEN COMPREHENSIVE FAQ

1. Where is The Haven located?

Omu-Epe, Lagos.

2. Who are the developers of Project The Haven?

Abode Assets Limited

3. What is the Title document on the land?

  • Survey
  • Deed of Assignment
  • C of O in view

4. Are there any encumbrances on the land?

None, all properties are duly verified from appropriate quarters.

5. What is the payment structure?

Special Promo is on; spread “Outright Payment” for 6 months.

Note: 500k is initial deposit.

6. What other payments are included aside from the price of the land?

None, the price is all-inclusive.

7. What infrastructure would the development fee cover?

  • Perimeter fencing
  • Land Clearing
  • Gate house
  • Land Parcellation
  • Road Stabilization

8. What documents would be given upon initial deposit?

  • Transaction Receipts
  • Contract of Sale

9. Is the road leading to the Estate Motorable?

Yes, paved and motorable leads to the estate

10. When would the plot be allocated?

Within 180 days of paying up to 50% of the total sum to be paid for the plot.

11. Any limit to commence construction?

Construction commences immediately once the building plan has been approved. (By Abode technical team and Govt regulatory agencies)

12. Can I resell my property?

Yes, The subscriber who pays up on their land can resell their plot.

However, the subscriber has to return all original documents and also come to the company with the new buyer to effect a change of ownership.

13. Are there any restrictions on building types?

No, however, approvals are necessary before construction begins

14. Is it dry land or a swampy area?

The land is 100% dry

15. What will I get as documentation?

  • Deed of Assignment
  • Survey Plan
  • Contract of Sale
  • Provisional Allocation
  • Transaction Receipt

16. What development will be done?

  • Perimeter Fencing
  • Gatehouse
  • Land Parcellation
  • Land clearing
