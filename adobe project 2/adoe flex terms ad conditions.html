Abode Flex Package.

The landed property made available by Abode Flex are available in different Estate locations across the States of the Federal Republic of Nigeria with varying cost of purchasing land. Hence, the sale cost per unit of the and available sizes differs and update prices are available on the company website

Monthly Instalment Payment/debit

Once you subscribe on the Website, you will be required to choose your mode of payment either through card or online transfer. You will also have the option to choose whether you want to be automatically debited monthly or you wish to make payment manually every time payment is due. Where you opt for manual payment, it is your responsibility to ensure that you do not fall into default, in order to avoid incurring default charges. We will equally try to remind you before the monthly deadline to ensure that you are well notified in a bid to forestall any default.

Duration of Payment

Depending on your choice, we have a payment plan that spans between 1 -3 years with varying prices. You are at liberty to subscribe at different times within the payment window. Each subscription shall be treated separately and the delivery date calculated from the date we receive the first instalment.

Default in monthly Payment/debit

The following provisions shall apply where default in payment occurs:

  • Where you default for two (2) cumulative months, you will be charged 10% of your monthly subscription for the two(2) months that you defaulted. Both your unpaid balance and accrued default charges must be paid before you can take delivery/possession of the subscribed property;
  • Where you fall into default for Four (4) cumulative months, your subscription shall be automatically terminated. TAKE NOTE that 60% of the amount paid shall be remitted to the Client in instalment, while we deduct a 40% administrative charge, PROVIDED THAT your accrued payment is up to 30% of the total subscription and if not, the same shall be forfeited. Your account will only be reactivated upon payment of the accumulated default charges, and you will ONLY continue payment at the prevailing market price per month for the property or outright;

Option To Take Possession Before Completion of Payment

Provided that you have been consistent with your monthly payment for the property, and you have completed 30% of the subscription price, you shall be granted Provisional Allocation and a Contract of Sale document shall be prepared and executed to evince grant of immediate possession, pending the completion of total payment for the subscribed property and your documents are delivered to you! Hence, you CANNOT sell or mortgage or lease the property to a third party without management approval.

Upon attaining 30% subscription payment either by outright deposit payment or by cumulative payments, your subscribed property unit(s) will be allocated to you within a month, and You shall be granted immediate provisional allocation between 30 - 90 days from the day you attain the status.

Effect of Default After Taking Possession Before Completion of Payment

  1. Where 30% deposit payment was made outright, you are still expected to continue your monthly payment for the period left to complete your subscription.

Termination by Death

In the event of death of a Subscriber, the next of kin shall notify the Company with the death certificate issued by a credible and certified hospital, as well as the grant of Probate or Letters of Administration in favor of the next of kin or other beneficiaries.

The beneficiaries of the estate of a deceased Subscriber shall have the following options:

  1. Effect a change of ownership, at no extra cost, and continue the payment for the property till completion; or
  2. Where payment for the property has already been completed by the deceased Subscriber, effect a change of ownership, at no extra cost; or
  3. Apply for termination of the subscription, in which case the rules relating to termination/request for refunds will apply;

Subscribers are advised to secure insurance cover for their subscription in case of death, to ensure that the beneficiaries can take benefit of the investment.

Change of Ownership/Termination

Change of ownership & Termination is permitted in the following situations:

  1. Where you are no longer able or interested in continuing with the payment of your subscription, you are at liberty to source for a new subscriber to take over the payment till completion
  2. Where you have completed your payment and taken possession and you subsequently wish to sell the property to a new subscriber;
  3. Inanyofthesituationslistedin(1)and(2)inthissectionabove,anadministrativechargeof5%of the total subscription fee shall be paid
  4. Where you are still paying for the unit(s) and any request for change of ownership is made after 30% of your subscription, the new buyer/subscriber shall pay at the prevailing rate. Where the location is closed, you shall contact us for the prevailing rate of the Estate. (in a situation where the location is closed, Abode Management determine present market price)
  5. In the event of termination, only subscribers who have paid 30% or more of the total amount due may request a refund (there will be no refund for amounts lower than 30%), subject to approval. However, a 40% administrative charge will be deducted from the refund. Subscribers who have not reached the 30% threshold will forfeit their initial deposit to cover administrative charges.

Change of Location

At any time before you are allocated your subscribed property , you may apply to change your subscription to another location. Your application will be reviewed and approved only on the following conditions:

  1. You must give a month's notice of your intention to apply for change of location before allocation.
  2. Availability of your property type in your preferred location;
  3. Payment of N30,000 as charge for change of location.

Change of Units

At any time before you are allocated your subscribed property you may apply to increase or reduce the number of your subscribed unit(s). Your application will be reviewed and approved only on the following conditions:

  1. Availability of your property size in your preferred location:
  2. In the case of reduction of units, you will be charged 5% of the total payments for the units to be changed.

Request for Corner Piece

You can ONLY make a request for a Corner Piece property where you have attained 75% payment of your subscription. You shall be charged the sum ( determined by Abode Management) or such other sum as may be communicated from time to time.

Property Management Charges

Property management charges for the following will apply upon taking immediate possession of your property:

  1. Security;
  2. Maintenance of common areas;
  3. Administrative charge.
